Hi, I’m Grace!

I’m a rising junior at Bates College in Lewiston, Maine.

Summer 2022

I’m so excited to be the Summer 2022 Dorothy W. Sanborn Intern at the Rufus Porter Museum of Art and Ingenuity in Bridgton, Maine! My responsibilities will include leading tours, organizing volunteers, and, most excitingly, using R to create interactive digital maps for an upcoming exhibit on the travels of Rufus Porter and other mid-19th century itinerant artists.

Course of Study
